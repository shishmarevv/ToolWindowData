Effect Size (Cliff's Delta)

What is this? Even if difference is significant, it's important to understand HOW LARGE it is.
Interpretation:
• |δ| < 0.147: negligible
• 0.147 ≤ |δ| < 0.33: small
• 0.33 ≤ |δ| < 0.474: medium
• |δ| ≥ 0.474: large
